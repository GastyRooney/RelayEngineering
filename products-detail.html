Solar Water Pump System

Solar Water Pump System is a sustainable and cost effective way to pump water using energy from the sun.

Types of Solar Water Pumps

1. Submersible Pumps: Installed deep in wells or water bodies, ideal for high-head applications.

2. Surface Pumps: Used for transporting water from lakes or rivers.

3. DC Pumps: Operate directly on solar-generated direct current from the sun.

4. AC Pumps: Use an inverter to convert solar power into alternating current to power the pump.

An On-Grid Solar System

An On-Grid Solar System is a solar power setup that is directly connected in the local electricity grid.

It allows users to generate their own electricity while also feeding excess power back into the grid.

How It Works

1. Solar panel absorb sunlight and convert it into electricity.

2. Inverter converts the DC electricity from the panels into Ac electricity for home or business use.

3. Grid connection ensures that any surplus energy is sent to the utility gird.

4. Net metering allows users to earn credits for excess electricity fed into the grid, reducing electricity bills.

A Carport Solar System

A Carport Solar System is a structure that integrates solar panels into a carport roof, allowing you to generate electircity while providing shade and protection for vehicles.

It's a great way to maximize space and harness renewable energy.

How It Works

1. Solar panels are installed on the carport roof to capture sunlight.

2. Inverters converts the solar energy into usable electricity.

3. Grid connection or battery storage allows excess energy to be stored or fed into the grid.

4. EV charging compatibility enables direct charging of electric vehicles.

A Hybrid Solar Sytem

A Hybrid Solar Sytem combines the benefits of both grid-tied and off-grid solar systems.

It includes solar panels, a hybrid inverter, and battery storage, allowing you to use solar energy efficiently while staying connected to the grid for backup power.

How It Works

1. Solar Panels generate electricity from sunlight.

2. Hybird inverters convertss DC electricity into AC for household use.

3. Battery storage saves excess energy for use at night or during power outages.

4. Grid connection provides backup power when solar and battery energy are insufficient.
